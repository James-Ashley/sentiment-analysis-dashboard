Washington, DC (CNN) Taylor Swift's song "Only the Young," is featured in a new ad from California Rep. Eric Swalwell encouraging young voters to get out to the polls in the final days of the 2020 campaign.  Swalwell tweeted the ad Friday with the message, "Thank you @taylorswift13, for voicing what #OnlyTheYoung can do. Let's run!"  Up there's the finish line.  Our future is worth our fight.
